About Chef Will

Where technique meets heart

Chef Will — William Gabriel Pereira — brings 16 years spanning the tech industry, restaurant management, and professional kitchens. A rare combination of business acumen and culinary mastery delivered to every table.

Classically trained, ServeSafe Certified, and bilingual in English and Spanish, Chef Will specializes in 15+ world cuisines. With deep personal experience living with Celiac disease (diagnosed 2001), Chef Will brings unparalleled expertise in gluten-free, vegan, allergen-safe cooking — not as an afterthought, but as a craft.
